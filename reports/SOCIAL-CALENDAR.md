## Content Production Notes

### Voice Guidelines for All Posts

**Do:**
- Write peer-to-peer (변리사가 변리사에게 / IP 전문가가 동료에게)
- Lead with a specific, concrete observation — not a generic statement
- Use real numbers wherever possible (85%, 10,000건, 5분, 90%)
- Acknowledge complexity — this is an expert audience, don't oversimplify
- End with a genuine question, not a formulaic CTA every time
- Keep paragraphs to 1–3 lines for LinkedIn readability

**Don't:**
- Use generic AI-content phrases ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- Overclaim ("혁명적", "완벽한")
- Post promotional content without educational value
- Use jargon without explaining it (some followers may be IP-adjacent, not core patent professionals)
- Post the same hook format twice in the same week
