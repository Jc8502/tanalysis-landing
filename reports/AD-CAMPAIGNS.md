## Campaign Strategy Overview

Tanalysis is a specialized AI patent tool built by patent attorneys (변리사) for IP professionals. The core differentiator is not generic AI — it is a patent-domain-specific model trained on actual Korean Patent Office and Intellectual Property Tribunal examination data, achieving 85% recall on examiner-cited prior art, 4x higher than competitors. With 80 million patents searchable in under 30 seconds and 90%+ classification accuracy, the product solves a real, painful workflow problem: patent attorneys and IP teams spend enormous time on repetitive, complex search queries that still produce incomplete results.

The ad strategy centers on three pillars: (1) attacking the pain of slow, inaccurate searches with hard proof (85% recall, 30-second search), (2) establishing credibility through the "built by patent attorneys" angle — the only model trained on KIPO/IPT rejection and invalidation data — and (3) driving free trial signups with zero friction (no credit card, 2-week team trial via team meeting). The free-tier entry point (20 points/day) lowers the conversion barrier significantly and should be the primary CTA for cold traffic.

LinkedIn is prioritized alongside Google because the target decision-makers — patent attorneys, in-house IP managers, and corporate IP strategists — are reachable by job title. Google captures high-intent search demand from IP professionals actively looking for patent search tools. Retargeting is critical given the B2B sales cycle: prospects will visit, evaluate, and return before converting. Display retargeting should focus on the government data voucher subsidy angle (최대 90% 지원, deadline 3/31) to create urgency.

## Audience Targeting

### Primary Audience Segments

**Segment A: Patent Attorneys & Law Firm Staff (변리사 / 특허법인)**
- Job titles: 변리사, Patent Attorney, IP Counsel, Patent Agent, 특허 담당자
- Company types: 특허법인, IP law firms, 법률사무소 (IP practice)
- Pain: Complex search formula construction, incomplete prior art search, time spent on invalidation searches
- Key message: 85% recall, trained on KIPO examination data, no complex search formulas

**Segment B: In-House IP Teams (인하우스 IP팀)**
- Job titles: IP Manager, IP Strategy, 지식재산팀, IP Legal, 특허팀장
- Company types: Large Korean corporates (삼성, LG, 현대 계열사 and mid-size tech companies), bio/pharma companies
- Pain: Portfolio management, FTO analysis, competitor patent monitoring at scale
- Key message: AI classification (10,000 patents in 5 minutes), weekly competitive intelligence report

**Segment C: R&D Teams and Research Institutes**
- Job titles: 책임연구원, Senior Researcher, R&D Manager, CTO, 기술전략
- Company types: Tech companies, automotive (자율주행), chemical, pharma, defense
- Pain: Missing prior art before filing, not knowing what competitors are patenting in their technical area
- Key message: Weekly competitive monitoring report, 80M patent database, Korean input searches full multilingual database

**Segment D: Corporate IP Strategy Managers**
- Job titles: IP Strategy Manager, Chief IP Officer, 기술경쟁력 담당, 전략기획
- Company types: Conglomerates, mid-large tech, VC/PE with patent portfolios
- Pain: No efficient system for competitive intelligence, reports too thick to read, delayed awareness of competitor IP moves
- Key message: Weekly 30-second briefing report, AI competitive signal interpretation, government subsidy available

## Campaign Notes & Strategic Recommendations

1. **Lead with the "built by patent attorneys" angle everywhere.** This is the most defensible trust signal and directly addresses the core fear of IP professionals: that generic AI tools don't understand patent law nuances.

2. **Use the "재현율 85%, 타사 대비 4배" stat as the primary number.** This is a concrete, verifiable claim that speaks directly to the practitioner's professional risk (missing prior art has real consequences for their clients).

3. **Government subsidy (데이터바우처) is a time-sensitive conversion accelerator.** With a March 31 deadline, urgency ads should be running immediately and should front-load this message in all SME-targeted campaigns.

4. **LinkedIn InMail outperforms for cold B2B outreach** in Korea when targeting named job titles. Prioritize InMail for patent attorney cold outreach where organic discovery is limited.

5. **Free tier lowers friction significantly.** Always include "무료로 시작하기" as an option — it removes the "evaluation risk" objection and gets users into the product where they can experience the quality firsthand.

6. **Team Meeting CTA is the highest-value conversion.** It signals organizational intent (not just individual curiosity), triggers the 2-week team trial, and gets a qualified sales conversation. Optimize retargeting specifically toward this CTA.

7. **Monitor for competitor brand terms.** If budget allows, consider a separate campaign targeting competitor brand keywords (키프리스, Espacenet, ThinkBig, etc.) with comparison-angle ads.

8. **Consider separate English-language campaigns** targeting Korean IP professionals who conduct international searches (US, EP prosecution) — they may respond well to English-language LinkedIn ads targeting "patent prosecution" skills.
